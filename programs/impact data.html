Daav — Organisation Timeline

Key milestones since founding

2019

Daav Founded

Organisation registered in Dar es Salaam with a focus on malaria care in Temeke district. First community health post opened.

2020

COVID-19 Response Pivot

Daav adapted malaria infrastructure for COVID-19 testing and isolation support, reaching 800 households during the pandemic peak.

2021

Mobile Clinic Programme Launched

First mobile unit deployed. Within 8 months, daily routes established across Temeke, Ilala, and Kinondoni districts.

2022

HIV/AIDS Programme & USAID Partnership

HIV testing and ARV adherence support launched with USAID technical partnership. 800 patients enrolled in first year.

2023

TB Programme & GiveWell Recognition

Community-based DOT programme launched. Daav shortlisted by GiveWell as a standout charity for cost-effectiveness research.

2024–25

Scale & Systems Strengthening

Third mobile unit deployed. Digital health records system launched. Target: 15,000 patients served by end of 2025.

Data Stories

Numbers With Names Behind Them

Impact data is most powerful when it's connected to real lives. These three stories illustrate what the numbers mean on the ground.

🦟 Malaria · Temeke

From Fever to School in 4 Days

Eight-year-old Amina was tested, diagnosed, and treated in a single morning at our Mtoni mobile clinic. She was back in school by Thursday — at a total programme cost of $4.20.

🫁 TB · Kinondoni

Six Months. One Worker. One Cure.

Hassan, a market trader diagnosed with TB in 2023, completed his full 6-month treatment course with daily DOT support from our community worker — avoiding MDR-TB and returning to work.

🎗 HIV · Ilala

91% Adherence: Behind the Number

Our 91% ARV adherence rate reflects 200+ patient navigators making daily check-in calls, organising medication pickups, and providing the quiet, consistent support that life-long treatment demands.
